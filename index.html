A NEW CODING HOPE

The purpose of this website is to hopefully act as a digital portfolio for my current and future illustration and graphic design work.

This desire has come from a life-long interest in art as a whole. From traditional Renaissance work to modern digital illustration and animation, I am all for learning about it (or at least trying to).

ABOUT ME

With my hopes and desires for this website’s future set aside, here are a few factoids about myself:

  1. I am a dual citizen of the USA & Canada by means of birth (though my family currently resides in Canada).
  2. I am currently a graphic design major, though I may add a Communications minor. We’ll see.
  3. I lived on the Graceland campus as a Hall Director’s kid for 8 years up until 2009.
  4. Aside from art, I enjoy baking, hiking, playing video and board games, and food in general.
  5. This is my second attempt at learning html.
