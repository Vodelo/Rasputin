"DANCERS NEEDED"

Created as part of a recruitment campaign, "Dancers Needed" was a flyer designed with the intent to draw interested dancers in to participate in a student music viedo project, while evoking the style of the music through visuals. The setting and quality is rustic urban, playing with near-complimentary colors to add an energy to the flyer that is evident in the music as well.

"MUSIC VIDEO SHOOT"

"Music Video Shoot" was created with the similar purpose and drive for "Dancers Needed". The blue was used for this flyer's backgorund in order to suggest the more laid-back work associated with the request being made. Similar elements to the "Dancers" are carried over, with some variation in color and industry imager to differentiate the purposes between the two campaign posters.

A CASE STUDY in DESIGN

The Case Study in Design is a collection of 5 graphic pieces with the innate goal of expressing the various elements and driving principles that make up graphic design. This page provides the description and explanation of the project, while using design elements carried throughout the project in order to develop consistency and matching messages.

"INFLUENCE"

The layout of "Influence" is inspired by the color wheel, and how the combination of colors in monochrome, analagous, and complimentary pairings can affect the viewer's emotions. It was also used to show how colors affect one another - toning the energies of one another, creating similarity or contrast in various ways.

"INTERSECTION": A FONT

A classroom project, this font set was required to take inspiration from the imagery used in its background. Varying boldness in the lines, as well as their criss-crossing patterns come from the catwalks sectioning the sky and walls of the downtown urban area, expresssing busyness and meticulous creation in one font.

"SOCIAL BUTTERFLIES"

This gif was developed for the purpose of providing a short story of 5-6 words within one image. "Social Butterflies" plays off of the social activity of the extroverted that creates buzz and pressure at parties, mixed with the phase "butterflies in your stomach", signifying the nervousness from peer pressure and anxiety in social college settings.
