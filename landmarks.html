LANDMARKS


Graceland University

First opended in 1895. A private university established by the Community of Christ, currently lead by President Patricia Draves.





Liberty Hall

Liberty Hall was once the home of Joseph Smith III. Mr. Smith helped establish the RLDS church (later the Community of Christ). The movmeent of the church to this area cause the establishment of the town.



Town Hall

Currently the Town Hall and police station, this building was once Lamoni's train station for the midwest. An old boxcar is posted on the sight for visitors to see. The building is often used as the stage for the town's annual Civil War Days weekend event.
