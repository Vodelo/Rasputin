CHASE WOOD


A dual citizen of the United States and Canada, Ms. Wood has used her experiences living with different people and environments to develop flexibility, atmosphere, and movement in her work. Ms. Wood is able to do business in both countries at this time, but looks to expand her availability to others in the global community.

She draws inspiration from the elements and principles of animation, while also deeply taking into consideration the atmospheres, characters, intentions, and qualities of a client's workplace and position to develop the best suited work possible.
